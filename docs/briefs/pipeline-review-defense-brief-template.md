This brief is not a forecast dashboard. It is a weekly evidence check: which deals are weak, why they are weak, and what action should happen this week.

## 6. Forecast Evidence

Use categories, not numeric scores.

### Defensible

Criteria:

- Recent customer interaction
- Clear decision owner or approver
- Confirmed next customer action
- Known objection status
- Evidence supports forecast timing

### Weak But Recoverable

Criteria:

- Real opportunity exists
- Some evidence exists
- One or two critical gaps remain
- Rescue action can happen this week

### Hope-Based

Criteria:

- Forecast depends on assumption
- Decision maker or timeline unknown
- No confirmed next customer action
- Follow-up is overdue or vague

### Unsupported

Criteria:

- No recent customer evidence
- No clear next action
- Account has gone silent
- Opportunity is based mostly on internal expectation

## 7. Manager Question List

Use these questions during self-review, rep review, or customer follow-up planning.

- Who owns the decision?
- What evidence supports this forecast?
- What is the next confirmed customer action?
- Which objection is still unpaid?
- What happens if no response this week?
- What is the latest customer signal?
- What is the procurement path?
- What technical proof does the customer still need?
- Is the next action ours, the customer's, or both?
- Should this deal be defended, downgraded, rescued, or removed?
