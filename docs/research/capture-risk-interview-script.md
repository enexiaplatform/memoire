# Capture Risk Interview Script

## Opening

Thanks for taking the time. I am researching how B2B salespeople keep track of customer context and follow-ups during long sales cycles.

This is not a product demo at first. I want to understand your real workflow before I show anything.

There are no right answers. I am looking for what you actually do, not what sounds ideal.

## Current Workflow Questions

Ask these before showing the demo.

1. What kind of customers or accounts do you manage?
2. How long is a typical sales cycle?
3. How many active accounts or opportunities are you usually tracking?
4. Where do you currently keep account context?
5. What tools do you use today?
6. How do you know who to follow up with each day?
7. How do you prepare before contacting an account again?
8. Tell me about a recent deal that went quiet or stalled. What happened?
9. What usually causes deals to go silent in your workflow?

## After-Call Behavior Questions

1. After a customer call or meeting, what do you usually do in the first 10 minutes?
2. Do you write notes immediately, later, or not at all?
3. Where do those notes go?
4. What details are most likely to get lost?
5. What follow-up commitments are easiest to forget?
6. How do you track objections or concerns raised by the customer?
7. How do you track who owns the decision and when they will decide?
8. How often do important next steps live only in your memory?

## Manual Capture Questions

1. If you had a tool that asked you to paste a short note after each important customer interaction, would you do it?
2. What would make that feel worth it?
3. What would make it feel like extra admin work?
4. How short would the capture flow need to be?
5. Would you use it for 7 days if it helped catch deals before they go silent?
6. Would you capture after every customer interaction, or only after important deals/calls?
7. What types of interactions would you actually capture?
8. Which interactions would you skip?
9. What would you expect the system to produce after you captured a note?

## Killer Question

Ask this directly and record the exact answer:

If Memoire only had manual capture during beta, would you use it daily?

Follow-ups:

1. Why or why not?
2. For how many days would you realistically try it?
3. What would make you stop?
4. What immediate reward would make manual capture feel worth it?

## Auto-Capture Source Questions

For each source, ask:

- Would you want Memoire to use this source?
- How much useful sales context lives here?
- How often would this source contain relevant information?
- What privacy or trust concerns would you have?
- Would you connect or provide this source during beta?

Sources:

1. Manual Quick Capture
2. Email thread import
3. Calendar meeting context
4. Meeting transcript import
5. Voice note capture
6. CRM import later

## Privacy / Trust Questions

1. What customer data would you not want a tool like this to access?
2. Would you be comfortable pasting selected email threads manually?
3. Would you be comfortable connecting email later if the system only processed selected threads?
4. Would you be comfortable importing meeting transcripts?
5. Would you prefer manual control over every captured item?
6. What would the product need to show you so you trust it?
7. What would make this feel risky or unacceptable?

## Ranking Exercise

Ask the user to rank capture sources from 1 to 6.

Sources:

- Manual Quick Capture
- Email thread import
- Calendar meeting context
- Meeting transcript import
- Voice note capture
- CRM import later

For the top 2 choices, ask:

1. Why did you rank this high?
2. What exact data would you expect it to capture?
3. Would you accept a lightweight prototype first?
4. What would make you stop using it?

For the bottom 2 choices, ask:

1. Why did you rank this low?
2. Is it a privacy issue, usefulness issue, or workflow issue?

## Demo Reaction Questions

Show the demo only after workflow, manual capture, and source ranking are complete.

Then ask:

1. If Memoire showed you "3 deals may go silent today," what would you expect to see underneath?
2. What proof would make you believe the system found a real risk?
3. Does seeing the stuck-deal queue make manual capture feel more worthwhile?
4. What would need to happen immediately after capture for you to keep using it?

## Closing Questions

1. Would you use this without auto-capture?
2. Would you use this with only manual capture for 7 days?
3. Which capture source should be tested first?
4. What would make you recommend this to another technical B2B salesperson?
5. Is there anything important about your workflow that I did not ask?
