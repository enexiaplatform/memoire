# Memoire Positioning

## One-Liner

Memoire is a personal Sales Memory System for people who sell without a sales team. It turns customer and client interactions into account memory, stuck-deal signals, next actions, and reusable sales knowledge.

## Core Contrast

Your CRM, spreadsheet, or notes track the record. Memoire remembers the story and what needs follow-up.

## What Memoire Is

- Personal Sales Memory System
- Daily Sales Action Engine
- Career Knowledge Vault for individual sellers and solo operators
- Private working layer for founder-led and solo-led sales

## What Memoire Is Not

- CRM clone
- Task manager
- Note-taking app
- Sales dashboard
- Generic AI chatbot
- Forecasting tool
- Business management app
- Invoicing, inventory, ecommerce, marketplace, or project-delivery system

## Target Users

- B2B sales professionals
- Account managers
- Business development managers
- Technical sales
- Consultative sellers
- Solo sales operators
- Founder-led sellers
- Consultants, freelancers, and agency owners selling client work
- Creators and experts selling sponsorships, partnerships, workshops, or services

## Persona Boundary

Use "solo operator" or "founder-led seller" when the user owns their own sales follow-up, opportunity memory, and deal story. Do not use C2B or C2C as the primary public persona because those labels are too broad and can pull the product toward marketplaces, ecommerce, or transaction management.

## Core Value

- Remember customer context
- Avoid missed follow-ups
- See stuck deals and broken loops
- Turn interactions into next actions
- Ask account-specific and opportunity-specific questions
- Learn from recurring sales patterns
- Prepare a review-ready deal story for yourself, a partner, a client, or a manager

## Product Loop

Capture -> Structure -> Memory -> Opportunity -> Action -> Ask -> Learning
